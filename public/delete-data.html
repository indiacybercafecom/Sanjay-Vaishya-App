Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Google Play's User Data policy, we provide a simple way for users to request the deletion of their account and any associated data.

How to Request Deletion

Since the Sanjay Vaishya App does not use a traditional login system, most user interactions are anonymous. However, if you have provided any personal information through our contact forms or other channels, you can request its deletion by following these steps:

  • Send an email to sanjayvaishya.dev@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request - [Your Name]
  • Include details of the information you would like us to remove.

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your request, our team will verify the details and process the deletion within 7-10 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the data has been permanently removed from our records.

Important Note

Please note that some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security, fraud prevention, or financial record-keeping).
